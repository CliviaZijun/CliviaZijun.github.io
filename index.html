Introduction

  • What is Art Toys

    Art Toys is usually regarded as a collection of adults over the age of 15 and has collection properties of artwork.

    They are either handmade by designers or made by independent toy companies.

    Because of technical and financial constraints, they are usually sold in limited quantities.

    They are not assembly-line products, but a condensation of the designer's abstract design thinking and ideas.

  • History

    1990s - Art Toys first appeared in Japan and China's Hong Kong.

    2000s - China's Taiwan and Hong Kong started holding Art toy exhibitions.

                 Art toys spreaded to China's mainland, Europe and America.

    2010s - More and more studios and IPs of art toys occured .

    So far - The influence of Art toys is getting wider and wider.

  • Materials

    Art toys has a wide variety of materials, among which the common materials can be classified into two categories: plastic and resin.

    Plastic: There are five main varieties of common general-purpose plastics: PE, PP, PVC, PS and ABS.
    Among them, PVC and ABS are often used in Art toys.

    Resin: The resin material of Art Toys are mostly PU resin, which is also a kind of plastic. It has some characteristics that the traditional five general purpose plastics do not have, so people prefer to distinguish it from other plastics.

    Each material has its own advantages, and designers will choose the material according to the shape of the toy.

Gallery

GUAGUA


IP Name: Guagua

Studio：ODDBOX Studio

IP Owner: Bob Tang

Designer：Bob Tang

Story: Guagua is based on an autistic child. After reaching out to an autistic child named "Guagua" at a charity event, the designer searched through documentaries and books to understand the pain, joy, and superior perception of people with autism. Guagua was created in the process of designer helping and observing "Guagua" and his family. At the same time, "Guagua" also let the designer find himself, the designer said that “Everyone is a lonely individual. People are eager to communicate but believe only what is on the surface, and are becoming less perceptive. From "Guagua", I learned how to communicate with my heart and it inspired my positive energy, which made me motivated to create and willing to pay more.”

About me

Me


I am a bit of an introvert.
Moreover, due to my strong ability of empathy, my emotions are easily affected by the emotions of others.

Therefore, socializing is an energy consuming thing for me. Although I may appear to outsiders as a lively person, only I know that after a social activity, I always need a quiet time alone to recharge.

At the same time, I am also a person who loves fantasy.
I often have strange ideas in my mind, and this makes my life a lot of fun.


My Passion


Because of the above characteristics, I'm more comfortable with nature, animals and artworks than with people.
They always immerse me in and make me have a better sense of who I am.

Among them, my favorite is artworks, especially Art Toys. They are like my childhood toys, which can bring me the purest joy and infinite imagination.
When I look at them, it's as if I'm writing a story about them, and it's as if I'm communicating with their designers in deep.
What's interesting is that everyone has different interpretations of one art toy, which is closely related to everyone's experience and personality.
Whenever I think of this, I can't help but marvel at the wonder of human beings.

Therefore, on my website, I want to share with you these wonderful artworks, that is, these kidult's toys.
In this increasingly impetuous world, hope they can bring you some inner peace.
